# Dortmund

'Dortmund' is a song composed and lyrics written by Richel Bilderbeek on
November 25th 2004.

Dortmund

Dortmund

## Background

I was going out in Dortmund with my research group. Suddenly,
these lyrics were in my head. A new song was born!
