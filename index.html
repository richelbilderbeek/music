    music¶

    All music on this website is encouraged to be downloaded and copied in their original form. It is not allowed to modify any of these files, except for making parodies. Copyright remains at the author(s) of the songs.

    Richel Bilderbeek enjoys making music with fun lyrics and a bad joke in the end. That he considers himself a bad singer and mediocre piano player does not hold him back in giving performances regularily.

    Formations¶

    The Bilderbeek Bilderbeek & Bogaart Bogaart Quartet¶

    'The Bilderbeek Bilderbeek & Bogaart Bogaart Quartet' was the band that consisted out of me and Geert van den Bogaart. I played two flutes (at the same time) and played the piano in Ode Aan Masculiniteit, where Geert did the piano in the other songs and did the vocals.

    The Fanstars¶

    'The Fanstars' was the band I was pianist of.
